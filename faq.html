FAQ

Questions buyers usually need answered before they book.

FAQ pages work best when they remove uncertainty without feeling dense. These answers are tuned to the questions Texas SMB buyers are most likely to ask first.

How long does a branding project usually take?

Starter projects move fastest. Growth and Authority take longer because they include more system depth, more assets, and more rollout support. Final timing depends on decision speed, asset readiness, and revision depth.

Do you only do logos?

No. The logo is only one part of the system. The focus is usable identity work that extends into templates, sales materials, web direction, and launch assets.

Will I own the final files?

Yes. Final approved assets are prepared for client use after the project is complete. Ownership and handoff expectations are confirmed during scoping.

Can you help connect the brand to a website later?

Yes. Website art direction and rollout support can be included as add-ons or scoped as part of a larger package when the website is part of the brand shift.

Why do you show pricing ranges instead of one flat number?

Because Texas SMB buyers want more clarity, but design scope still varies based on stage, deliverables, revision depth, and timeline. The ranges make the buying path clearer without pretending every brand project is identical.

What happens after I reach out?

You get a reply with fit confirmation, likely package direction, and next-step timing. The process is designed so you never have to guess what comes next.
